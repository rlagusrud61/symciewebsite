Location

We are happy to announce that Symposium Zephyrus will be held physically at Kinepolis! Kinepolis located near the Enschede Kennispark Station, and only 10 minutes away by bike from the University.

Directions

From campus / From Enschede

1. From the main entrance of the campus (Drienerlolaan), drive along the Hengelosestraat towards the direction of Enschede Kennispark (or Hengelo).

2. Turn left on Capitool and reach the Enschede Kennispark station (or Grolsch Veste stadium).

3. Turn right until you see Kinepolis.

Entrance

This is where you can find the entrance to our symposium!
